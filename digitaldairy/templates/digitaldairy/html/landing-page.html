App Features

Digital Farm Data Storage

With our easy to use and customise software solutions, secure storage of generated farm data such as milk production and distribution, animal breeding, animal health, animal feeding, employee management and farm finance is made easy.

Data Retrieval

We make it easy for farms to securely retrieve and export stored data to other formats. Through guaranteed integrity of the data, insight from the data is used in making important decisions and strategies.

Analytics

Our software products provide analytics and statistics generated from stored data such as milk production and distribution, breeding and farm finance. Farm statistics are important in getting a general view of farm activities.

Farm Activities Tracking

Through tracking of various farm activities such as animal breeding and health, milk production and distribution, farm income and expenses we assist farms diagnose and observe trends in these activities and take appropriate action.

Important Farm Alerts

Our software products have breeding, health, feeding alert modules that give you the dairy farmer, important alerts on the important activities of your farm such as breeding, health and feeding.
